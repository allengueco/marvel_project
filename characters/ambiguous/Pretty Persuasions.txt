Franklin''') is a fictional [[mutate (fictional)|mutate]] character appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]]. The character first appeared in ''New Warriors'' vol. 1 #4. She was a member of [[Psionex]].\n\n==Fictional character biography==\nLittle is known about Pretty Persuasions's past before joining up with Psionex. She used to be an [[exotic dancer]] until being operated upon by Genetech, a company that creates super-beings. Genetech has trouble keeping her in line as she tended to seduce her minders, both male and female{{Citation needed|date=January 2010}}. As part of Psionex, she fights the [[New Warriors]]; they were soundly defeated.<ref>''New Warriors'' #4</ref> Later, feeling like they are nothing more than lab specimens, the team escapes Genetech<ref>''New Warriors'' #15-16</ref> and Persuasions returns to work at a New York City [[strip club]]. [[Nova (Richard Rider)|Richard Rider]] and Vance Astrovik make a trip to check on her.<ref>''New Warriors'' #18</ref>\n\nShe later joins a new version of Psionex, which reforms under the new [[Asylum (comics)|Asylum]] (formerly the villain known as Darkling). This teams dedicates itself to crime-fighting but falls apart after a civilian with a heart condition dies during an encounter. Persuasions returns to Genetech for observation but later appears again as an exotic dancer.{{Issue|date=July 2009}}\n\n===''Civil War''===\n{{Main|Civil War (comics)}}\n\nDuring the Civil War event, Pretty Persuasions is apprehended by Baron Zemo and his [[Thunderbolts (comics)|Thunderbolts]]. She is shown imprisoned in energy rings, with other villains, having been captured long before the Thunderbolts were given official allowance to take down villains.<ref>''Thunderbolts'' #103</ref> She faces the 'choice' of working as an agent of the government, or going to jail for not registering with the Superhuman Registration Act and her past crimes. Persuasions, along with many other recruits, are forced into aiding the Thunderbolts and Earth's heroes in a battle against the cosmic powered [[Grandmaster (Marvel Comics)|Grandmaster]] and dozens of super-powered opponents. The recruits are seen being overwhelmed and their final fate is not yet made clear.{{Issue|date=July 2009}}\n\n===Dark Reign===\nPretty Persuasions and Psionex are revealed as members of the Initiative's Maryland team.<ref>''Avengers: The Initiative'' #26</ref>\n\n== Powers and abilities==\nPretty Persuasions can stimulate a person's pleasure centers psionically, meaning she can excite, as well as to increase the erotic  urges/sexual desires of others. With her power she can seduce and control other people, usually men.
