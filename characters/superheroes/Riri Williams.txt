She started attending the [[Massachusetts Institute of Technology]] on scholarship.<ref name="Invincible Iron Man #12">\'\'Invincible Iron Man\'\' Vol. 3 #12</ref> Working alone, Riri designs a suit of armor similar to the [[Iron Man\'s armor|Iron Man armor]] using material stolen from campus. When campus security knocks at her door, she dons the suit and escapes.<ref>\'\'Invincible Iron Man\'\' Vol. 3 #7</ref>\n\nWhen Williams prevents two inmates from escaping the New Mexico State Penitentiary, her suit is damaged.<ref>\'\'Invincible Iron Man\'\' Vol. 3 #11</ref>\n\nUpon returning to her mother\'s house, Riri continued to work on improving the suit much to the dismay of her aunt. [[Iron Man|Tony Stark]] hears of Riri\'s accomplishment and goes to meet her. During their meeting, Tony Stark states to Riri that he will endorse her decision to be a superhero.<ref name="Invincible Iron Man #12"/>\n\nAppearing in her Rescue armor following the "Civil War II" storyline, [[Pepper Potts]] confronted Riri Williams and her Tony Stark A.I. in an attempt to tell Riri the problems of being a superhero only for them to be attacked by Techno Golem and her Biohack Ninjas.<ref>\'\'Invincible Iron Man\'\' Vol. 4 #3</ref> As Riri flees, Pepper fights against Techno Golem and her Biohack Ninjas as Techno Golem tries to get answers from Pepper on how she knows Riri.<ref>\'\'Invincible Iron Man\'\' Vol. 4 #4</ref> When Techo Golem\'s armor breaks and Tomoe tries to attack Riri, Pepper fires her Rescue armor\'s gauntlets at Tomoe which knocked her out. Upon Sharon Carter formally meeting Riri after the arrest of Tomoe and the Biohack Ninjas, Pepper states to Riri that they will talk again as she flies off in her Rescue armor.<ref>\'\'Invincible Iron Man\'\' Vol. 4 #5</ref> Pepper Potts was present with Mary Jane Watson, Friday, the Tony Stark A.I., and Tony Stark\'s biological mother Amanda Armstrong when they are in the Hall of Armor where Riri voices her knowledge of each of the Iron Man armors. When Amanda Armstrong offers to have Riri let Tony Stark\'s labs be her base of operations, Riri is hesitant as Pepper encourages her. The next day at her home, Riri\'s family is visited by the headmistress at M.I.T. who would like Riri to continue working there as it has gotten sloppy since she left. In addition, Riri will be allowed to use the laboratories there. After her work in one of the laboratories, Riri has the Tony Stark A.I. find her something to blow off some steam on. The Tony Stark A.I. finds [[Armadillo (comics)|Armadillo]] on a crime spree enabling Riri as Ironheart to defeat Armadillo. Afterwards, she is approached by the [[Champions (2016 team)|Champions]] who offer her membership into their group.<ref>\'\'Invincible Iron Man\'\' Vol. 4 #6</ref>\n\nDuring the \'\'[[Secret Empire (comics)|Secret Empire]]\'\' storyline, Ironheart is seen fighting the Army of Evil at the time of Hydra\'s takeover of the United States. After [[Helmut Zemo|Baron Helmut Zemo]] has [[Blackout (Marcus Daniels)|Blackout]] surround Manhattan with [[Darkforce]] upon enhancing him with the powers of the [[Darkhold]], Riri has the Tony Stark A.I. to send a distress signal to all available heroes to meet her in Washington DC as fast as they can. Following Hydra\'s takeover, Ironheart and [[Falcon (comics)|Falcon]] II join up with the Champions when they assist the Underground. She and the Champions later followed [[Black Widow (Natasha Romanova)|Black Widow]] when she makes her own plans for Captain America. During their training, the young heroes argue over Black Widow\'s brutality and mercilessness. During a mission, the heroes infiltrate a Hydra base to find someone crucial to Black Widow\'s plan. They\'re later told by Black Widow that they are going to kill Steve Rogers, after Hydra attacked the Underground\'s hideout and destroyed it. In Washington DC, at the beginning of their assault, Spider-Man begins to fight Captain America when Black Widow intervenes and gets killed. Just as Spider-Man is about to kill Steve Rogers, the others convince him to not do it and get arrested.<ref>\'\'Secret Empire\'\' #0-7</ref> She also helped the Champions in searching for survivors
