She first appeared in \'\'[[Doc Samson]] #1\'\' (Jan 1996), and was created by [[Dan Slott]] and [[Ken Lashley]].\n\n==Fictional character biography==\nDelilah "Dee Dee" Dearborn was a student at [[Empire State University]] who gains superhuman powers from an accident with a gamma accelerator at the school.<ref>\'\'Doc Samson\'\' #1</ref> [[Doc Samson]] mistakes her for a murderer and they fight.<ref>\'\'Doc Samson\'\' #2</ref> She later helps him track and confront the real killer.<ref>\'\'Doc Samson\'\' #3-4</ref>\n\n===Initiative===\nGeiger is seen at the military base [[Camp Hammond (comics)|Camp Hammond]], as an official recruit for the [[Avengers: The Initiative|Initiative]], which is a super-human military draft. She is introduced to Camp Hammond along with [[Ant-Man (Eric O\'Grady)|Ant-Man]], [[Crusader (Marvel Comics)#Crusader (Skrull)|Crusader]], [[Melee (comics)|Melee]], [[Geldoff]], [[Dragon Lord (comics)#Tako Shamara|Dragon Lord]], [[Red Nine]], and [[Diamondback (Rachel Leighton)|Diamondback]].<ref>\'\'Avengers: The Initiative\'\' #8</ref>\n\nShe was one of the trainees that attacked [[Michael Van Patrick#KIA|KIA]] and was defeated.<ref>\'\'Avengers: The Initiative\'\' #10</ref>\n\n===Dark Reign===\nDuring the \'\'[[Dark Reign (comics)|Dark Reign]]\'\' storyline, Geiger graduated
