Created by [[Mark Waid]] and [[Alan Davis]], the character first appeared in \'\'Free Comic Book Day 2016 Civil War II\'\' (July 2016).\n\n==Publication history==\n{{expand section|date=June 2018}}\nNadia van Dyne briefly had her own comic, \'\'The Unstoppable Wasp\'\', but it was cancelled after eight issues. The series is scheduled to return on October 3rd, 2018.<ref>https://www.newsarama.com/40337-unstoppable-wasp-back-from-cancellation-with-new-ongoing.html</ref>\n\n==Fictional character biography==\nNadia is the child of [[Hank Pym]] and his first wife, Maria Trovaya, who was abducted and supposedly killed by foreign agents. Nadia was raised in the [[Red Room (comics)|Red Room]] until she obtained a Pym Particle sample and escaped.<ref name="Nadia">\'\'All-New, All-Different Avengers\'\' #9</ref> Initially intending to meet her father, Nadia learns that he is more or less dead, and instead uses parts of his costume and other materials to create a Wasp suit,<ref>\'\'Free Comic Book Day Avengers 2016\'\'</ref> hoping to obtain the admiration of her father\'s allies.<ref name="Nadia" /> She later meets [[Wasp (comics)|Janet van Dyne]], and they get along quite well, with Janet feeling that she has the potential to become a true hero.<ref>\'\'All-New, All-Different Avengers\'\' #12</ref><ref>\'\'All-New, All-Different Avengers\'\' #14</ref>\n\nNadia soon joins the [[Avengers (comics)|Avengers]] and gains [[Citizenship of the United States|U.S. citizenship]]. Upon realizing that [[S.H.I.E.L.D.]]\'s index of the world\'s most intelligent people doesn\'t list any women above 27th place, she starts the program G.I.R.L. (Genius In action Research Labs) to look for women with genius intellects.<ref>\'\'The Unstoppable Wasp\'\' #1-2</ref> When choosing a legal name, Nadia takes the surname \'van Dyne\', as she does not know much about her birth parents and Janet has believed in and supported her.<ref>\'\'The Unstoppable Wasp\'\' #8</ref>\n\nAt one point she aids [[Ant-Man (Scott Lang)|Scott Lang]] - who is in outer space at that time - in an attempt to return to Earth, which results in a sequence of bizarre adventures in the microverse. However, in the process the two of them became [[Quantum entanglement|quantum-entangled]] with each other.<ref>\'\'Ant-Man and the Wasp\'\' #1-5 (June-August 2018)</ref>\n\n==Powers and abilities==\nNadia uses Pym particles to alter her size; she can shrink to a minimum of a few centimeters or grow to a maximum of several hundred feet.
