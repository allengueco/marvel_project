He is a [[Sorcerer Supreme]] in the [[Earth-691]] timeline of the fictional [[Marvel Universe]].\n\n==Publication history==\n{{expand section|date=May 2017}}\nKrugarr first appeared in \'\'Guardians of the Galaxy Annual\'\' #1 and was created by [[Jim Valentino]].\n\n==Fictional character biography==\nIn the 22nd century, [[Doctor Strange]] finds a [[List of alien races in Marvel Comics#L|Lem]] named Krugarr on the planet [[List of planets in Marvel Comics#Lemista|Lemista]]. He agrees to become Strange\'s apprentice and learn the ways of magic. Krugarr succeeds Strange as Sorcerer Supreme.<ref>\'\'Guardians of the Galaxy Annual\'\' #1</ref>\n\nStrange, who takes on the title of the [[Ancient One]], is later slain by the villain [[Dormammu]].<ref>\'\'Guardians of the Galaxy\'\' #36-37</ref>\n\nKrugarr takes on [[Talon (Marvel Comics)|Talon]] as an apprentice. Talon later declares that Krugarr was forced to reject him for not being able to keep up with his studies. Despite this, the two entities remain friends. Talon attitributes this to being a \'nice flake\'. Despite the lack of studying, Krugarr has managed to teach Talon simple magics, such as levitation.<ref>\'\'Guardians of the Galaxy\'\' #18-20</ref>\n\nDuring a battle with a murderous gang of vigilantes, [[Vance Astro|Major Victory]] is shot in the head. [[Wonder Man|Hollywood]] brings him to Krugarr, who manages to save his life. During this incident, they share a psychic link, which lasts long after Major Victory is healed.{{issue|date=May 2017}}\n\nSome time later, the world that is controlled by the entity [[Mainframe (comics)#Earth-691|Mainframe]] is rocked with disasters due to the interference of an ancient Earth virus. Krugarr is summoned by [[Martinex]] via the highly-advanced \'Star\' worn by Guardians and their allies. Krugarr, who had just established his Sanctum Sanctorum on his homeworld of Lem, regretfully declines because there is a crisis growing in the \'\'Dark Dimensions\'\'. Krugarr\'s telepathically sends Hollywood to help. Hollywood joins with several other powerful superheroes to save the innocent survivors of Mainframe\'s world. This spurs the creation of the [[Galactic Guardians]].{{issue|date=May 2017}}\n\n==Powers and abilities==\nAs a Sorcerer Surpreme, Krugarr has the ability to perform great many magical spells such as mystic bolts, flight, astral projection, and calling upon various magical entities.\n\n==Other versions==\nThe Earth-616 version of Krugarr appeared during the \'\'[[The Infinity War|Infinity War]]\'\' storyline as a member of the Mourners.<ref>\'\'Quasar\'\' #39.
