She is a member of the [[Inhumans]].\n\n== Fictional character biography ==\n\nNahrees was part of the delegation sent to Earth in 1992. The delegation consisted of [[Tonaja (comics)|Tonaja]] (team leader), [[San (comics)|San]], [[Alaris (comics)|Alaris]], Nahrees and [[Jolen (comics)|Jolen]], Nahrees\' former lover.{{issue|date=January 2013}} During this time, she studied at the [[University of Wisconsin\xe2\x80\x93Madison]]. She dated Damon Hart, a rich and spoiled human.<ref>\'\'Inhumans\'\' (vol. 4) #9</ref> Nahrees is depicted throughout the series as being arrogant, self-centered, and cold hearted.\n\nShe works as a Royal Guard and her base of operation is Attilan. \n\nShe took part in a raid against the United States during the Silent War.{{issue|date=January 2013}} The raid was botched by the [[Fantastic Four]] and she was captured by ONE.{{issue|date=January 2013}}\n\nHer enemies are Richard Anderson, Doctor Cartwright, [[Fantastic Four]], [[Invisible Woman]] and [[Quicksilver (comics)|Quicksilver]].\n\n== Powers and abilities ==\n\nNahrees can generate massive quantities of electricity, possibly up to ten million volts.{{citation needed|date=December 2014}} She manipulates the electricity naturally found in the environment and is able to store and absorb a seemingly limitless amount of current. She uses electricity for gliding. \n\nShe also has the physiology of an average Inhuman, so all her bodily functions are superior to that of a normal human in strength, reaction time, stamina, resistance to injury and speed.
