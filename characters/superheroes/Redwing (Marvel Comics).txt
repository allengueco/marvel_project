The Nazi [[Red Skull]] uses the [[Cosmic Cube]] to connect Sam and Redwing by a [[Telepathy|mental link]]. Wilson and Redwing then become allies of [[Captain America]].<ref>\'\'Captain America\'\' #186</ref> Redwing has been by Falcon\'s side ever since, fighting villains such as [[Grey Gargoyle]], [[Viper (Madame Hydra)|Viper]] and [[Diamondhead (comics)|Diamondhead]]. Redwing stayed with Falcon when he became the new Captain America.<ref>\'\'All-New Captain America\'\' #1</ref>\n\n==Powers and abilities==\nIn his earliest appearances Redwing exhibits a close bond with Sam Wilson, which is confirmed as being a telepathic link by [[Professor X]] in \'\'Captain America\'\' #174 .
