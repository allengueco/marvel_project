b"{{About|comic book character|the moth genus|Elysius (moth)}}\n\n{{Infobox comics character <!--Wikipedia:WikiProject Comics--> \n|image=\n|caption=\n|character_name=Elysius\n|real_name=\n|species=\n|publisher=[[Marvel Comics]]\n|debut=''[[Mar-Vell|Captain Marvel]] #59'' (Nov 1978)\n|creators=[[Doug Moench]] (writer)<br>[[Pat Broderick]] (artist)\n|alliances=\n|aliases=Mistress of Paradise\n|powers=\n}} \n \n'''Elysius''' is a fictional character appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]].\n\nElysius is an artificially created being possessing the attributes of an [[Eternal (comics)|Eternal]].  She became the lover of [[Mar-Vell]], and after his death used his genetic material to impregnate herself with [[Genis-Vell]] and [[Phyla-Vell]].\n\n==Fictional character biography==\nElysius was created by [[ISAAC (comics)|ISAAC]], the sentient computer system of [[Titan (Marvel Comics)|Titan]]. She serves as ISAAC's lieutenant, and aided ISAAC in its conquest of Titan.<ref>''Captain Marvel'' #59-60</ref>\n\nShe later aided [[Captain Mar-Vell]], and [[Drax the Destroyer]] against [[ISAAC (comics)|ISAAC]], [[Stellarax]], Lord Gaea, and Chaos. She then began her romance with Mar-Vell.<ref>''Captain Marvel'' #61-62; ''Marvel Spotlight'' Vol. 2 #1-2</ref> She returned to Earth with Mar-Vell and [[Rick Jones (comics)|Rick Jones]].<ref>''Marvel Spotlight'' Vol. 2 #3</ref>\n\nShe later accompanied Mar-Vell to Denver to visit Rick Jones.<ref>''Incredible Hulk'' Vol. 2 #245</ref> She accompanied [[Betty Ross Banner|Betty Ross]], Rick Jones, and Fred Sloan to Gamma Base in search of Mar-Vell and the [[Hulk (comics)|Hulk]]. She departed Gamma Base with Mar-Vell.<ref>''Incredible Hulk'' Vol. 2 #247-248</ref>\n\nSome time later, Elysius attended the death-watch of Captain Mar-Vell.<ref>''Marvel Graphic Novel'' #1</ref>\n\nLater, she remained on Titan when [[Starfox (comics)|Starfox]] departed for Earth to join the [[Avengers (comics)|Avengers]].<ref>''Avengers'' #230</ref>\n\n==Powers and abilities==\nElysius is an artificially created being patterned on and possessing the attributes of the long-lived offshoot of humanity called the [[Eternal (comics)|Eternals]].  She has superhuman stamina, and her strength, speed, and durability are higher than that of a human. Elysius possesses an extremely long lifespan and immunity to all terrestrial diseases.\n\nElysius has limited telepathic abilities of an unrevealed nature.  She keeps griffin-like creatures which obey her telepathic commands.\n\nShe uses an alien hand-weapon capable of disrupting force fields or of firing energy of an unspecified nature as bursts of concussive force.  [[ISAAC (comics)|ISAAC]] crafted her personal sky-ship, which resembles an ancient Earth galleon but possesses the capacity of flight.\n\n==References==\n{{reflist}}\n\n==External links==\n*http://en.marveldatabase.com/Elysius\n\n{{Eternals}}\n\n[[Category:Characters created by Doug Moench]]\n[[Category:Characters created by Pat Broderick]]\n[[Category:Comics characters introduced in 1978]]\n[[Category:Marvel Comics Eternals]]\n[[Category:Marvel Comics characters who can move at superhuman speeds]]\n[[Category:Marvel Comics characters with superhuman strength]]\n[[Category:Marvel Comics superheroes]]\n[[Category:Marvel Comics telepaths]]"
