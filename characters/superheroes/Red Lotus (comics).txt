When Gow was murdered, Red Lotus was led to believe that the culprit was [[Gambit (comics)|Gambit]] by the Examiner, who wanted to gain control of the Triad for himself.<ref>''X-Treme X-Men'' #5-9</ref>\n\nRed Lotus assisted the X-Treme X-Men team against [[Sebastian Shaw (comics)|Sebastian Shaw]] and [[Lady Mastermind]] after the truth was revealed, and later helped them while they were trying to repel an interdimensional invasion in [[Madripoor]].<ref>''X-Treme X-Men'' #10-18</ref>\n\nAfter the invasion on Madripoor, Paul became an ally to [[Viper (Marvel Comics)|Viper]] and joined her undercover at the [[Hellfire Club (comics)|Hellfire Club]] hoping to shut down their mutant slave ring. He was almost murdered by [[Selene (comics)|Selene]], but Marvel Girl was able to save him.{{Issue|date=August 2009}}\n\nHe was last seen still as an ally to Courtney Ross, Viper, Sunspot and their new Hellfire Club, however, what happened to him after M-Day still remains a mystery.{{Issue|date=August 2009}}\n\n==Powers and abilities==\nRed Lotus is a superhuman martial artist who possesses enhanced strength, speed, reflexes, agility, dexterity, coordination, balance, and endurance.\n\n==In other media==\nRed Lotus is set to make an appearance in ''[[Dark Phoenix (film)|Dark Phoenix]]'' played by [[Andrew Stehlin]].
