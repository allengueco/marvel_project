Her first appearance was in ''Uncanny X-Men #235''.\n\n==Fictional character biography==\n\nThe super-strong mutant known only as Punchout was a member of the super-powered [[Press Gang (comics)|Press Gang]], law enforcers, from the island nation of [[Genosha]].\n\nAfter repeated disruptions to the Genoshan government, the island fell into a civil war.{{Issue|date=May 2010}} Punchout stayed with a small group of Press Gang members, fighting against the rebels. In one battle, she was knocked down by [[Jennifer Ransome]].{{Issue|date=May 2010}}\n\nWith the genocide of the entire Genoshan population by [[Sentinel (comics)|Sentinels]] controlled by [[Cassandra Nova]],{{Issue|date=May 2010}} Punchout's final fate is unknown.
