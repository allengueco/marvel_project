He was defeated by the combined forces of T\xe2\x80\x99Challa and [[Spider-Man]].<ref>\'\'Marvel Team-Up\'\' #87</ref>\n\nHellrazor was caught without his weapons at the "Bar With No Name," and shot dead by the [[Scourge of the Underworld]].<ref>{{cite comic|writer = [[Mark Gruenwald]]|penciller = [[Paul Neary]]|inker = [[Dennis Janke]]|story = Overkill|title = [[Captain America]]|volume = 1|issue = 319|date = July 1986|publisher = Marvel Comics}}</ref>\n\n==Powers and abilities==\nHellrazor can draw upon any energy source to replenish his strength and stamina.\n\nHellrazor\xe2\x80\x99s primary weapons were a pair of wrist blades \xe2\x80\x93 bracelets which had sharpened edges, and could also fire a stream of sharp razors from his wrists.\n\n==Violator==\n===Civil War===\nAnother character named Hellrazor has appeared as a [[Registration acts (comics)#2006 Superhuman Registration Act|Superhuman Registration Act]] violator. He was running a protection racket in Billings, Montana, and was being held as a prisoner of the [[Thunderbolts (comics)|Thunderbolts]]. Two days after his arrest, he encountered [[Robbie Baldwin|Penance]].  Hellrazor began to taunt Penance about his role in the Stamford incident, until Penance attacked him and beat him severely.<ref>\'\'Thunderbolts\'\' #116</ref>\n\nIt is unknown at this time what connection, if any, he has to the Caprice, [[Mind-Wave#Caged Angels|Mindwave]], [[Mirage (Marvel Comics)#Caged Angels|Mirage]], and [[Blue Streak (comics)#Caged Angels|Bluestreak]], who were also held as prisoners of the Thunderbolts shortly thereafter.\n\n==References==\n{{reflist}}\n\n== External links==\n*{{Marvunapp|http://www.marvunapp.com/Appendix/hellrazo.htm|Hellrazor (Denton Phelps)}}\n*[http://marvel.wikia.com/wiki/Denton_Phelps_%28Earth-616%29 Hellrazor (Denton Phelps)] at the Marvel Database wiki\n*{{comicbookdb|type=character|id=14454|title=Hellrazor (Denton Phelps)}}\n*{{comicbookdb|type=character|id=18933|title=Hellrazor II}}\n\n[[Category:Characters created by Gene Colan]]\n[[Category:Characters created by Mike Deodato]]\n[[Category:Characters created by Warren Ellis]]\n[[Category:Comics characters introduced in 1979]]\n[[Category:Comics characters introduced in 2007]]\n[[Category:Marvel Comics characters with superhuman strength]]\n[[Category:Marvel Comics mutates]]\n[[Category:Marvel Comics supervillains]]'
