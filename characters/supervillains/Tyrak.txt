He has served in a number of missions for Attuma, and has fought the [[Avengers (comics)|Avengers]] on more than one occasion.\n\nHe posed as the [[Inhumans|Inhuman]] [[Triton (comics)|Triton]] to capture the Avengers for Attuma.<ref>''Avengers'' #154</ref> Tyrak battled the Avengers when they arrived to battle Attuma.<ref>''Avengers'' #156</ref> He later fought the Avengers again, attempting to regain his lost honor.<ref>''Avengers'' #172</ref>\n\nSome time later, Tyrak returned to try to defeat [[Namor]] to regain Attuma's favor, but was defeated by the Avengers.<ref>''Avengers'' #278</ref> Attuma later dispatched Tyrak during the [[Atlantis Attacks]] storyline, but he was again defeated by the Avengers and their allies.<ref>''Avengers Annual'' #18</ref> Tyrak later fought the Avengers and the [[People's Protectorate]].<ref>''Avengers'' #319-323</ref>\n\nDuring the ''[[Fear Itself (comics)|Fear Itself]]'' storyline, Tyrak helps Attuma (in the form of Nerkodd: Breaker of Oceans), Attuma' sister Aradnea, and [[Tiger Shark (Marvel Comics)|Tiger Shark]] take over New Atlantis and launch an attack on the surface world.<ref>''Fear Itself: The Deep'' #1</ref>\n\n==Powers and abilities==\nHe rivals Attuma's strength and is able to enhance it further by increasing his height to a maximum of {{convert|40|ft|m}}.\n\n==References==\n{{reflist}}\n\n==External links==\n* [http://marvel.wikia.com/wiki/Tyrak_%28Earth-616%29 Tyrak] at Marvel Wiki\n* [http://comicvine.gamespot.com/tyrak/4005-28674/ Tyrak] at Comic Vine\n* {{Marvunapp|http://www.marvunapp.com/Appendix2/tyrakatlan.htm|Tyrak}}\n\n{{Namor}}\n\n[[Category:Characters created by George P\xc3\xa9rez]]\n[[Category:Characters created by Gerry Conway]]\n[[Category:Fictional secret agents and spies]]\n[[Category:Marvel Comics Atlanteans (Homo mermanus)]]\n[[Category:Marvel Comics characters with superhuman strength]]\n[[Category:Marvel Comics supervillains]]\n\n\n{{Marvel-villain-stub}}"
