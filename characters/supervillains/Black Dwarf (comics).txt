He is a prominent member of the [[Black Order (comics)|Black Order]], a team of aliens who work for the titan [[Thanos]].\n\nBlack Dwarf appears as Cull Obsidian, one of the Children of Thanos, in the 2018 film \'\'[[Avengers: Infinity War]]\'\', portrayed by [[Terry Notary]].\n\n==Publication history==\nBlack Dwarf first appeared in a one panel cameo in \'\'[[The New Avengers (comics)|New Avengers]]\'\' vol. 3 #8 and was created by [[Jonathan Hickman]] and [[Mike Deodato]]. His full appearance, along with a number of the other members of the Black Order, takes place in \'\'[[Infinity (comic book)|Infinity]]\'\' #1, released in October 2013.\n\n==Fictional character biography==\nBlack Dwarf is a member of [[Thanos]]\' [[Black Order (comics)|Black Order]] where he is the powerhouse of the Mad Titan\'s army.<ref>\'\'Infinity\'\' #1</ref>\n\nWhen Thanos targeted [[Earth]] as the next planet he would raze during the [[Infinity (comics)|Infinity]], Black Dwarf arrived in [[Wakanda (comics)|Wakanda]].<ref>\'\'New Avengers\'\' vol. 3 #8</ref> To his surprise, Black Dwarf found great resistance in that country and was forced to retreat. For his failure, Thanos expelled Black Dwarf from the Black Order.<ref>\'\'New Avengers\'\' vol. 3 #10</ref>\n\nThanos gave Black Dwarf one more chance to prove himself by sending him to protect The Peak and keep it from being reclaimed by the [[Avengers (comics)|Avengers]] following their fight against the Builders.<ref>\'\'Avengers\'\' vol. 5 #22</ref> During the fight against the Avengers, Black Dwarf was killed by [[Ronan the Accuser]].<ref>\'\'Avengers\'\' vol. 5 #23</ref>\n\nDuring the "No Surrender" arc, Black Dwarf was resurrected by [[Challenger (comics)|Challenger]] who reassembles the Black Order in order to go into a contest against [[Grandmaster (Marvel Comics)|Grandmaster]]\'s [[Lethal Legion]].<ref>\'\'Avengers\'\' #681. Marvel Comics.</ref>\n\n==Powers and abilities==\nBlack Dwarf has super-strength, enhanced density, and his skin is impenetrable.\n\n==In other media==\n===Television===\n* Black Dwarf appears in \'\'[[Avengers Assemble (TV series)|Avengers Assemble]]\'\', but has no dialogue.<ref name="Voice">{{cite web|url=http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/characters/Marvel-Universe/Black-Dwarf/|title=Voice of Black Dwarf|website=Behind the Voice Actors|accessdate=April 29, 2018}}</ref> In the show\'s continuity, he is once again part of Thanos\' Black Order.
