During the battle Torgo was seriously wounded, and left for dead.  He was then buried alive, and was then found and slain by a Vampire.  Torgo then became a vampire himself.  When [[Dracula (Marvel Comics)|Dracula]] was transformed from vampire to human by [[Satan]] (actually [[Mephisto (comics)|Mephisto]]), Torgo became the new Lord of the Vampires.  As Lord of the Vampires, he had mental control over all other vampires, and he was physically powerful in comparison even to other vampires.  Torgo took control of Earth\'s vampires, and began planning to conquer the world.  However, Dracula regained his vampiric powers.  He challenged Torgo to a duel and destroyed him, resuming leadership of the world\'s vampires.
