She is a mentally unstable omnipath and telepathic parasite who seeks out intellect to devour.<ref>\'\'Infinity\'\' #1. Marvel Comics.</ref>\n\nIn the search for Thanos\' son [[Thane (comics)|Thane]] during the \'\'[[Infinity (comic book)|Infinity]]\'\' storyline, Supergiant and [[Corvus Glaive]] laid siege to the [[X-Mansion|Jean Grey School for Higher Learning]] and easily defeated the [[X-Men]]. They left after realizing that Thane was not there.<ref>\'\'New Avengers\'\' vol. 3 #9. Marvel Comics.</ref>\n\nWhen the Black Order seized [[Wakanda (comics)|Wakanda]], Supergiant was left in control of [[Black Bolt]] who she would mentally order to activate the [[Illuminati (comics)|Illuminati]]\'s hidden bombs in Wakanda\'s Necropolis.<ref>\'\'Infinity\'\' #5</ref> When the Illuminati arrived to save Black Bolt, Supergiant used Black Bolt\'s ability to defeat the heroes. Upon activating the bomb, Supergiant was faced by [[Maximus (comics)|Maximus]] who had the trigger. Maximus triggered the bomb, but also used [[Lockjaw (comics)|Lockjaw]] to transport Supergiant along with the bomb to a distant uninhabited planet where she perished in the explosion of the bomb.<ref>\'\'Infinity\'\' #6. Marvel Comics.</ref>\n\nDuring the "No Surrender" arc, Supergiant came back as a psychic projection, along with the rest of the Black Order, thanks to the [[Challenger (comics)|Challenger]] who had set them up against [[Grandmaster (Marvel Comics)|Grandmaster]]\'s [[Lethal Legion]].<ref>\'\'Avengers\'\' #676. Marvel Comics.</ref> During the battle in Antarctica, the Black Order retreat after [[Corvus Glaive]] is killed. Supergiant stays to take control of [[Jane Foster (comics)|Thor]], but is dissipated by Lethal Legion member Ferene the Other.<ref>\'\'The Avengers\'\' #680. Marvel Comics</ref>\n\n==Powers and abilities==\nSupergiant has telepathic abilities that allow her to mind-control anyone and feed on their intellect.\n\n==In other media==\n===Television===\n* Supergiant appears in the \'\'[[Avengers Assemble (TV series)|Avengers Assemble]]\'\' animated series, voiced by [[Hynden Walch]]. She is seen in the episodes "New Frontier" and "Avengers World". \n* Supergiant appears in the \'\'[[Guardians of the Galaxy (TV series)|Guardians of the Galaxy]]\'\' animated series, voiced again by Hynden Walch. This version has a size-shifting ability and one of Star-Lord\'s ex-girlfriends. In the episode "Undercover Angle", the [[Nova Corps]] had previously apprehended Supergiant at the time when the [[Guardians of the Galaxy (2008 team)|Guardians of the Galaxy]] were infiltrating the Nova Corps HQ posing as Nova Corpsman. When Supergiant was accidentally released, she fought Star-Lord, [[Drax the Destroyer]], [[Rocket Raccoon]], and [[Groot]] until she was knocked out by [[Gamora]].
