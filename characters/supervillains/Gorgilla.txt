After Gorgilla saves the expedition from a dinosaur, a \'\'[[Tyrannosaurus|Tyrannosaurus rex]]\'\', they in gratitude decide to leave the island and never mention Gorgilla.<ref>\'\'Tales To Astonish\'\' #12 (vol. 1, Oct. 1960)</ref>\n\nGorgilla, however, becomes curious about mankind and stows away on a ship bound for New York. Gorgilla stops an assassination attempt while in New York, but dies from a fall from the Statue of Liberty.<ref>\'\'Tales of Suspense\'\' #18 (vol. 1, Jun. 1961)</ref>\n\nThis is later corrected and it is revealed that the adventurer [[Doctor Druid]] talks Gorgilla down from the Statue of Liberty and ensures that he is returned to Borneo.<ref>\'\'Marvel Universe\'\' #4 (1998)</ref>\n\nGorgilla is among those summoned by [[Yandroth]] and influenced into attacking the [[Defenders (comics)|Defenders]]. Gorgilla dropped a building on the Avengers, but is defeated by the Defenders.<ref>\'\'Defenders\'\' vol. 2 #1 (2001)</ref>\n\nGorgilla is later deposited on [[Monster Isle]] by the [[Fantastic Four]] and eventually undergoes a rehabilitation program, which involves him being shrunken down to human size and hypnotically stripped of his powers so as to allow him to enter human society. Gorgilla takes up a job as a window cleaner at the Baxter Building and later joins fellow monsters [[Fin Fang Foom]], [[Elektro (comics)|Elektro]], and [[Googam]] as part of the "Fin Fang Four" to defeat the microscopic alien conqueror [[Tim Boo Baa]].<ref>\'\'[[Fin Fang Four]]\'\' #1 (2005)</ref>\n\nDuring the \'\'[[Monsters Unleashed (comics)|Monsters Unleashed]]\'\' storyline, Gorgilla, Fin Fang Foom, Green Thing, and Zzutak confront Kei Kawade in the forest outside his house where they caught him offguard.<ref>\'\'Monsters Unleashed\'\' Vol. 2 #1</ref>\n\n==Powers and abilities==\nGorgilla has super-strength, agility and durability.\n\n==References==\n{{reflist}}\n\n==External links==\n* [http://marvel.com/universe/Gorgilla Gorgilla] at Marvel.com\n* [http://marvel.wikia.com/Gorgilla_%28Earth-616%29 Gorgilla] at Marvel Wiki\n* [http://www.comicvine.com/gorgilla/4005-22528/ Gorgilla] at Comic Vine\n* {{comicbookdb|type=character|id=1954|title=Gorgilla}}\n\n{{Eternals}}\n\n[[Category:Comics characters introduced in 1960]]\n[[Category:Characters created by Stan Lee]]\n[[Category:Characters created by Jack Kirby]]\n[[Category:Fictional apes]]\n[[Category:Marvel Comics characters with superhuman strength]]\n[[Category:Marvel Comics supervillains]]\n\n{{Marvel-villain-stub}}'
