DeMatteis]] and [[Paul Neary]].\n\nThe character subsequently appears in \'\'Captain America\'\' #298-299 (October-November 1984), and #301 (January 1985). The character subsequently appears as \'\'\'Slash\'\'\' in #355-357 (July-September 1989), and \'\'[[I Heart Marvel|I \xe2\x99\xa5 Marvel: Outlaw Love]]\'\' (April 2006).\n\nSlash appeared as part of the "Sisters of Sin" entry in the \'\'[[Official Handbook of the Marvel Universe|Official Handbook of the Marvel Universe Update \'89]]\'\' #7.\n\n==Fictional character biography==\nA member of the Sisters of Sin, Slash was a young disciple of the [[Red Skull]].  Her physical age was accelerated into an old woman, calling herself Sister Agony because of the razor-sharp talons she had in place of her hands.  She and her Sisters attacked [[Captain America]] but were defeated.{{issue|date=December 2012}} She was eventually restored to her natural age.  However, soon after, she returned alongside the Sisters of Sin, this time as a young adult.  She and her sisters were once again defeated by Captain America.{{issue|date=December 2012}}\n\nShe was later seen at a bar with the [[Answer (comics)|Answer]], playing strip games with her sisters.<ref>\'\'[[I Heart Marvel|I \xe2\x99\xa5 Marvel: Outlaw Love]]\'\' (April 2006)</ref>\n\n==Powers and abilities==\nSlash wields razor-sharp metal claws and is well-trained in battle.
