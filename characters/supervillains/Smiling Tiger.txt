Art by Mark Bagley.\n|character_name=Smiling Tiger\n|real_name=Conrad Mack\n|publisher=[[Marvel Comics]]\n|debut=[[New Warriors]] (1st series) #19\n|creators=[[Fabian Nicieza]] & [[Mark Bagley]]\n|alliances=[[Folding Circle]]<br/>[[Thunderbolts (comics)|Thunderbolts]]\n|aliases=\n|powers=razor sharp claws.\n}}\n'''Smiling Tiger''' (Conrad Mack) is a [[Marvel Comics]] supervillain. \n\n==Fictional character biography==\nDuring the [[Vietnam war]], Smiling Tiger's father Collier Mack served in the Army recon unit nicknamed the Half-Fulls along with [[Left Hand (comics)|Diego Casseas]] and the fathers of the other members of the [[Folding Circle]]. After stumbling upon the Dragon's Breadth cult in Cambodia, the soldiers encountered Tai, who then demanded they marry the six remaining daughters of the Dragon's Breadth's breeding program.<ref>New Warriors 23-24</ref>\n\nWhether Collier or his wife played a part in Smiling Tiger's childhood is unknown. Smiling Tiger appears to have long blades in place of his fingernails and toenails. He is mute and tends to act as if he is a [[feral]] animal. His degree of intelligence has never been explored.\n\nSmiling Tiger was recruited by the Left Hand to join the Folding Circle in his bid to gain the power of the Dragon's Breadth cult. After a confrontation with Tai and the [[New Warriors]], the Folding Circle stole the [[List of vehicles in Marvel Comics#Quinjet|Quinjet]] that the New Warriors had originally stolen from the [[Avengers (comics)|Avengers]]. The Folding Circle crashed in [[Madripoor]] and tried to become players in the Madripoor underworld.<ref>Night Thrasher #3-4</ref>\n\n===Civil War/The Initiative===\nDuring the [[Civil War (comics)|Civil War]] event, Smiling Tiger was forced to join the [[Thunderbolts (comics)|Thunderbolts]].<ref>Thunderbolts 103-104, 107</ref>\n\nSmiling Tiger has been identified as one of the 142 registered superheroes who appear on the cover of the comic book ''[[Avengers: The Initiative]]'' #1. <ref>[http://www.marvel.com/news/comicstories.947 Avengers: The Initiative #1 Character Map]</ref>\n\n==Universal Wellspring==\nSmiling Tiger is a [[Mutate (comics)|Mutate]] who like the majority of the [[Folding Circle]] are descended from a cult whose members have tapped into the energy of the Universal Wellspring for centuries.\n\n==References==\n{{reflist}}\n\n{{New Warriors}}\n\n[[Category:Characters created by Fabian Nicieza]]\n[[Category:Marvel Comics mutates]]\n[[Category:Marvel Comics supervillains]]\n\n{{Marvel-villain-stub}}"
