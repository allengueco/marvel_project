Asmodeus is an extradimensional [[List of Marvel Comics demons|demon]], subordinate to [[Mephisto (comics)|Mephisto]], who has clashed on numerous occasions with the first [[Ghost Rider (Johnny Blaze)|Ghost Rider]].\n\n==Publication history==\nAsmodeus first appeared in \'\'[[Ghost Rider]]\'\' #53 (February 1981), and was created by [[Michael Fleisher]] and [[Don Perlin]].\n\nThe character subsequently appeared in \'\'Ghost Rider\'\' #64 (January 1982), and #76 (January 1983).\n\n==Fictional character biography==\nIn his first appearance, Asmodeus sends the demon Tabicantra to manipulate and otherwise destroy Ghost Rider and gain control over [[Zarathos]]. Tabicantra eventually turns against Asmodeus, helping the hero free himself from the demonic influence.<ref>\'\'Ghost Rider\'\' #53, 1981</ref>\n\nAsmodeus later captured Ghost Rider, but failed to gain control over Zarathos once again.<ref>\'\'Ghost Rider\'\' #64, 1982</ref> Later, Asmodeus attempted to trick [[Mephisto (comics)|Mephisto]] into gaining control of Zarathos. However, Asmodeus failed and was seemingly destroyed by Mephisto.<ref>\'\'Ghost Rider\'\' #76, 1983</ref>\n\n[[Saturnine (comics)|Saturnine]] is a servant of Asmodeus, and a fellow enemy of Zarathos and Blaze.\n\nDuring the "[[Fear Itself (comics)|Fear Itself]]" storyline, Asmodeus attended the Devil\'s Advocacy where they talked about the [[Serpent (comics)|Serpent]]\'s threat on Earth.<ref>\'\'Journey Into Mystery\'\' #627</ref>\n\nUpon infiltrating the Cellar and distracting [[Spider-Man (Miles Morales)|Spider-Man]] II, [[Old Man Logan]] enters the cell of Asmodeus and makes him an offer to help him get back to Earth-807128 in order to rescue Bruce Banner Jr. before he becomes the Warlord.<ref>\'\'Old Man Logan\'\' Vol. 2 #19</ref> Asmodeus states to Logan that he would help him in his mission if he picked up some of his things. Afterwards, Asmodeus begins to help Logan in his mission to rescue Bruce Banner Jr.<ref>\'\'Old Man Logan\'\' Vol. 2 #20</ref>\n\n==Powers and abilities==\nAsmodeus can shoot mystic energy blasts and has limited mind control. While Asmodeus is immune to disease and aging, he can be injured by other demons.\n\n==Other characters named Asmodeus==\n[[Image:AsmodeusC.jpg|thumb|left|Asmodeus, servant of [[Satannish]].]]\nOver the years, there have been several minor characters with the name "Asmodeus" appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]].
