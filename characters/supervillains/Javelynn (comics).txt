b"{{Superherobox\n|image=Javelynn.jpg\n|image_size=100\n|caption=Javelynn, Art by [[Scott Kolins]]\n|character_name=Javelynn\n|real_name=\n|publisher=[[Marvel Comics]]\n|debut=''[[Hawkeye (comics)|Hawkeye]]'' vol. 2 #1 (1994)\n|creators=[[Chuck Dixon]]<br>[[Scott Kolins]]\n|alliances=[[Secret Empire (organization)|Secret Empire]]\n|powers=-Deadly accuracy in use of miniature javelins<br/>-Excellent athlete and gymnast<br/>-Hand-to-hand combat skills\n}}\n\n'''Javelynn''' is a fictional supervillain appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]]. The character first appeared in ''Hawkeye'' vol.2 #1 (1994) and was created by [[Chuck Dixon]] and [[Scott Kolins]].\n\nThe correct spelling, '''Javelin''', is the name of two separate fictional superheroes in the [[Marvel Universe]].\n\n==Publication history==\nJavelynn appeared as one of the central villains in ''[[Hawkeye (comics)|Hawkeye]]'' vol. 2 #1-4 (1994).\n\n==Fictional character biography==\nJavelynn was hired along with [[Trick Shot (comics)|Trick Shot]] as mercenaries to serve a cell of the subversive organization known the [[Secret Empire (organization)|Secret Empire]]. [[Viper (Madame Hydra)|Viper]], the leader of the cell, ordered Javelynn and Trick Shot to protect the animal experiments which were being conducted at the Secret Empire headquarters, the Enclave, in the [[Canadian Rockies]]. Sometime later, the Enclave was stumbled upon by [[Hawkeye (comics)|Hawkeye]], who had isolated himself in the area after the apparent death of his wife [[Mockingbird (Marvel Comics)|Mockingbird]]. Hawkeye battled with Javelynn and his former mentor but was quickly overpowered and driven away. Reporting back to [[Viper (Madame Hydra)|Viper]], Javelynn and Trick Shot were ordered to hunt down and kill Hawkeye and anyone who got in their way.<ref>''Hawkeye'' vol. 2 #1 (1994)</ref> Later, after Trick Shot had decided to abandon his mercenary contract and side with his former pupil, Javelynn battled Hawkeye alone and was effortlessly subdued.<ref>''Hawkeye'' vol. 2 #4 (1994)</ref>\n\n==Powers and abilities==\nJavelynn displays a deadly accuracy with projectile weapons. Her weapons of choice are miniature javelin-like stakes, which are visibly stored around her wrists, ankles, and her back. Javelynn also demonstrates an athletic and acrobatic prowess as well as honed hand-to-hand combat skills.\n\n==Javelin (Darnin)==\n{{main|Spaceknights}}\n'''Javelin (Darnin)''' is a member of the [[Spaceknights]] and an ally of [[Rom (comics)|Rom]]. Javelin uses a plandanium javelin as his primary weapon.<ref>''Rom'' #26 (Jan 1982)</ref>\n\n==Javelin (S.H.E.)==\n'''Javelin''' is a [[Finland|Finnish]] superhero and a member of '''S.H.E.''' ([[Super Heroes of Europe]]) from the 2000 [[Sentry (Robert Reynolds)|Sentry]] limited series.
